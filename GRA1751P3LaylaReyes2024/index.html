Glass Blocs • 80's Covers

SUN, OCT 27 @ 8 pm

Glass Blocs is a band of five South Florida musicians with a shared affinity for classic '80s British/Euro new wave music with a Miami flavor. The band features lead singer/guitarist Chris Donaghy, who moved to Miami in 2015. He's joined by lead guitarist Steve Harriman and synthesizer player Jason Zack, bassist Tony Schreiber and drummer Louis LaFontisee.
